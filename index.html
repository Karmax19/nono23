Project Proposal: Bhutan - The Land of Happiness

In this solo personal project, I aim to enhance the existing Bhutan website, celebrating the country's unique blend of ancient traditions and modern transitions. Bhutan, nestled in the eastern Himalayas, exudes captivating beauty and cultural richness.

Project Goals

The primary goals of this project are:

  • Create an engaging website that transports visitors to Bhutan's serene monasteries, breathtaking landscapes, and spiritual depth.
  • Provide articles, images, and videos that educate users about Bhutan's history, traditions, and landmarks.

Scope of Work

The scope includes:

  • Refining existing HTML code for improved structure and readability.
  • Enhancing content with descriptive text and relevant images.
  • Ensuring cross-browser compatibility and responsive design.
